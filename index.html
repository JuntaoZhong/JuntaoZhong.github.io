About Me:

I'm an international student in Carleton College (Class of 2023), MN, US. I'm interested in a lot of different things (that's why I go to a liberal arts college): I love Computer Science and Biology; I enjoy wrestling, weight lifting, and jogging; I play the accordion and sing baritone.

Before high school, I lived in Foshan, China (a city near HongKong). I then went to high school in Seattle. I speak Mandarin, Cantonese, Hakka(客家话), currently learning Spanish.

For summer 2020, I conduct research on cotton-top tamarins (Saguinus oedipus) at Dr. Julie Neiworth's Primate Cognition Lab . They voluntarily participate (rewarded with treats) in cognition studies, which includes inferential reasoning (cups study), delayed matching-to-sample, aging and alzheimer research, etc.

I throughly clean the cages weekly and monkeys enjoy nutrious monkey food and fresh fruits (organic cherries, strawberries, bananas etc.) that even I am jealous of.
